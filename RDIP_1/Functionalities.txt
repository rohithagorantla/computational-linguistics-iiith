List of Functionalities

Drop down menu : to select required corpus
 
on selecting required a paragraph is displayed
We have to calculate number of tokens and types present over there.

tokens and types : 
Enter the tokens and types you calculated in the given boxes and then click submit

Submit:
if the entered tokens and types are correct background is displayed green otherwise red.
It displays right answer if both number of tokens and types are correct and also a button continue is displayed
other wise it shows wrong answer.

continue: 
we have to recalculate new types present in that corpus and then click on submit.
it gives right answer if we enter correct value otherwise wrong answer.
